open computing jobs in 2024


According to the Bureau of Labor and Statistics, the number of open jobs in computing are projected to rise by 12.5% by 2024, double the average rate. That's more opportunities than the population of Alaska.

Jobs in the computing field are consistently ranked among the highest paying in the United States. The average software engineer makes $98,260 a year.
